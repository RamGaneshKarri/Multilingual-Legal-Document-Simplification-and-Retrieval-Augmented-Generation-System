AGREEMENT FOR SALE

This Agreement for Sale is made on 15th January 2024 between Mr. Rajesh Kumar (hereinafter referred to as "the Seller") and Ms. Priya Sharma (hereinafter referred to as "the Buyer").

WHEREAS the Seller is the absolute owner of the property situated at Plot No. 123, Sector 45, Gurgaon, Haryana, measuring 200 square yards (hereinafter referred to as "the Property").

AND WHEREAS the Buyer has agreed to purchase the Property from the Seller for a total consideration of Rs. 50,00,000 (Rupees Fifty Lakhs only).

NOW THIS AGREEMENT WITNESSETH AS FOLLOWS:

1. The Seller agrees to sell and the Buyer agrees to purchase the Property for the consideration mentioned above.

2. The Buyer shall pay the amount in the following manner:
   a) Rs. 10,00,000 as advance on signing this agreement
   b) Rs. 40,00,000 on or before 30th March 2024

3. The sale shall be completed on or before 15th April 2024 as per the provisions of the Transfer of Property Act, 1882.

4. The Seller warrants that the Property is free from all encumbrances and he has clear and marketable title to the Property.

5. In case of breach of this agreement by either party, the aggrieved party shall be entitled to claim damages as per Section 73 of the Indian Contract Act, 1872.

6. Any dispute arising out of this agreement shall be subject to the jurisdiction of the Courts at Gurgaon only.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF the parties have signed this agreement on the date mentioned above.

Seller: Rajesh Kumar
Buyer: Priya Sharma
